Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

Interactive Power BI dashboard for retail sales insights and business performance analysis.

Overview

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to transform retail sales data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard helps monitor sales performance, customer trends, product categories, and regional performance through dynamic visualizations.

The Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to identify sales trends, high-performing products, and customer purchasing behavior from large datasets, making business decisions more difficult.

Approach

01

Cleaned and transformed retail sales data using Power Query.

02

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard with KPIs, charts, slicers, and filters.

03

Generated actionable insights to support business decision-making and performance tracking.

Tech Stack

Power BI Excel DAX Power Query

Outcome

Created an interactive business intelligence dashboard that enables stakeholders to analyze sales performance, identify top-performing products, monitor revenue trends, and make data-driven decisions efficiently.
